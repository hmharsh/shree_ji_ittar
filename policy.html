Policies for online store

# Shipping policy

  • We strive to process all orders within 1-3 business days.
  • Unless the order is placed by any marketplace, we primarily ship orders with help of Shiprocket.
  • Generally, deliveries can take up to a week of time.
  • Once the shipment is picked up a Shiprocket generated currior-tracking link will be auto shared on Swiggy minis chat.
  • The currior tracking link will show the live shipment updated along with estimated delivery date.
  • Shipping costs will be calculated at checkout based on the weight and dimensions of your order and your delivery address.
  • We may offer free shipping on orders above a certain value.
  • Delivery times are estimates and may be subject to delays due to unforeseen circumstances, such as weather conditions, holidays, or carrier issues.
  • We are not responsible for any delays caused by customs clearance or other factors outside of our control.

# Cancellation policy

  • Full refund if you cancel before order is accepted by us.
  • For any queries on cancellation reach out to us.

# Return policy

  • We have a no-return policy. However, if your item arrives damaged during transit, please notify us within 24 hours of receipt. If possible, please include an unboxing video to document the damage.
  • In the case of damaged items, we will only offer an exchange. We cannot accept returns for any other reason.
