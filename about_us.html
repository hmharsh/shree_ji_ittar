About

What Inspire Us

Listen🔊 in :- Hindi, English

We noticed many people love 💕 fragrances,
but there weren't many pure ittars specifically made for temples and prayer 🥺.

We believe these holy places deserve beautiful, undiluted scents, so we set out to create them!

Unlike Regular devotional ittars which often contain chemicals for faster skin absorption or for stronger scents, we were looking for special ittars meant for truly devotional purposes that didn't contain such harsh chemicals. And thus 🪔 began the start of Shree Ji Ittar to fill the air with beautiful natural scents.
But there was another thought – Affordability.

We wanted our ittars to be affordable. So, instead of diluting the ittar, we kept the quality high and simply used plain roll-on bottles & simple packaging📦.

Shree Ji Ittar became a symbol of both devotion and affordability😇, a reminder that 'even the simplest things, offered with love, can uplift the spirit.'
